NutrientKG: A Food-Health Knowledge Graph for Disease-Aware Recipe Adaptation

Current Version: 1.0 (CKD Instantiation) | Release Date: May 2026 | License: CC-BY 4.0 | DOI: Pending (Zenodo)

Overview

NutrientKG is a food-health knowledge graph providing infrastructure for disease-aware recipe adaptation across chronic disease conditions. This initial version instantiates NutrientKG for Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD), integrating culinary knowledge, nutritional data, validated substitution pairs, and disease staging constraints.

Key Capabilities:

  • Nutritional queries over 467,069 USDA FoodData Central entries
  • Validated substitution recommendations with population-level frequency data
  • Disease-diet constraint checking using KDIGO 2024 guidelines
  • Progression-aware dietary safety verification across CKD stages

Key Properties

Namespaces:

  • Entities: http://nutrientkg.org/entity/
  • Properties: http://nutrientkg.org/property/ (prefix: nkgp:)

Recipe-Ingredient Relationships:

  • nkgp:has_Ingredient - Links recipes to ingredients
  • nkgp:has_amount_grams - Ingredient quantity

Nutritional Properties:

  • nkgp:has_usda_entry - Links ingredients to USDA nutritional data
  • nkgp:sodium_mg - Sodium content (mg/100g)
  • nkgp:potassium_mg - Potassium content (mg/100g)
  • nkgp:phosphorus_mg - Phosphorus content (mg/100g)
  • nkgp:protein_g - Protein content (g/100g)
  • nkgp:phosphorus_source_type - Classification (animal/plant/additive)

Substitution Properties:

  • nkgp:substitutes_for - Direct ingredient-to-ingredient substitution link
  • nkgp:substitution_source - SubstitutionPair → source ingredient
  • nkgp:substitution_target - SubstitutionPair → target ingredient
  • nkgp:substitution_frequency - Observation count in Recipe1M

Disease Constraint Properties:

  • nkgp:safe_at - Ingredient safe at given CKD stage
  • nkgp:restricted_at - Ingredient violates constraints at given stage
  • nkgp:has_ckd_risk - Links ingredient to RiskProfile violation details
  • nkgp:safeForStage - NKF recipe validated for specific stages

CKD Stage Threshold Properties:

  • nkgp:sodium_threshold_mg - Per-meal sodium limit (mg)
  • nkgp:potassium_threshold_mg - Per-meal potassium limit (mg)
  • nkgp:phosphorus_threshold_mg - Per-meal phosphorus limit (mg)
  • nkgp:protein_threshold_g - Per-meal protein limit (g)
  • nkgp:guideline_source - Clinical guideline version (KDIGO_2024)
  • nkgp:stage_order - Numeric stage ordering for progression queries

KDIGO 2024 Per-Meal Thresholds

Thresholds are derived by dividing KDIGO 2024 daily limits by three meals:

Nutrient Stage 1 Stage 2 Stage 3a Stage 3b Stage 4 Stage 5
Sodium (mg) 767 767 500 500 333 267
Potassium (mg) — — — 667 500 500
Phosphorus - animal/additive (mg) — — — 233 167 167
Phosphorus - plant (mg) — — — — 167 167
Protein (g) — — 19 14 14 9

Dashes indicate nutrient not actively restricted at that stage.

Sample SPARQL Queries

Q1: Substitutes by Frequency (Tier 1 - Culinary Knowledge)

Retrieve validated substitutes for butter ranked by observation frequency:

PREFIX nkg: <http://nutrientkg.org/entity/>
PREFIX nkgp: <http://nutrientkg.org/property/>

SELECT ?sub ?freq WHERE {
  ?ing rdfs:label "butter"@en .
  ?pair rdf:type nkg:SubstitutionPair ;
        nkgp:substitution_source ?ing ;
        nkgp:substitution_target ?sub ;
        nkgp:substitution_frequency ?freq .
}
ORDER BY DESC(?freq)

Results: olive oil (547), margarine (320), coconut oil (156)

Q2: Recipe Nutrient Profile (Tier 1)

Get complete nutrient profile for all ingredients in a recipe:

PREFIX nkg: <http://nutrientkg.org/entity/>
PREFIX nkgp: <http://nutrientkg.org/property/>

SELECT ?ing ?sodium ?potassium ?phosphorus ?protein WHERE {
  ?recipe rdfs:label "chicken stir fry"@en ;
          nkgp:has_Ingredient ?ing .
  ?ing nkgp:has_usda_entry ?usda .
  ?usda nkgp:sodium_mg ?sodium ;
        nkgp:potassium_mg ?potassium ;
        nkgp:phosphorus_mg ?phosphorus ;
        nkgp:protein_g ?protein .
}

Q5: Recipe Violations at Stage (Tier 2 - CKD Constraints)

Identify ingredients violating KDIGO constraints at Stage 3b:

PREFIX nkg: <http://nutrientkg.org/entity/>
PREFIX nkgp: <http://nutrientkg.org/property/>

SELECT ?ing ?risk_type WHERE {
  ?recipe rdfs:label "chicken stir fry"@en ;
          nkgp:has_Ingredient ?ing .
  ?ing nkgp:has_ckd_risk ?risk .
  ?risk nkgp:at_stage nkg:stage_3b ;
        nkgp:risk_type ?risk_type .
}

Results: soy sauce (914mg sodium vs 500mg limit), chicken breast (228mg phosphorus vs 233mg), chicken broth (343mg sodium)

Q6: Safe Across Progression (Tier 3 - Both Knowledge Types)

Find substitutes safe across all stages from 3b onward:

PREFIX nkg: <http://nutrientkg.org/entity/>
PREFIX nkgp: <http://nutrientkg.org/property/>

SELECT ?sub (COUNT(?pair) AS ?freq) WHERE {
  ?ing rdfs:label "chicken"@en .
  ?pair rdf:type nkg:SubstitutionPair ;
        nkgp:substitution_source ?ing ;
        nkgp:substitution_target ?sub .
  MINUS {
    ?stage a nkg:CKDStage ;
           nkgp:stage_order ?ord .
    FILTER(?ord >= 3)
    ?sub nkgp:has_usda_entry ?usda .
    ?usda nkgp:sodium_mg ?sodium .
    ?stage nkgp:sodium_threshold_mg ?lim .
    FILTER(?sodium > ?lim)
  }
}
GROUP BY ?sub
ORDER BY DESC(?freq)

Results: turkey (13), canned chicken (5)
